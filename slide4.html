Lengkuas Island is also synonymous with the lighthouse tower inherited from the Netherlands which has stood firm to this day. The 52 meter high lighthouse was built in 1882 by ZM Willem III. Visitors can climb the lighthouse with an entrance fee of IDR 5,000. From the lighthouse tower, they can enjoy views of the high seas and ships passing by towards the South China Sea. For information, from November to December 2021, Belitung is predicted to be crowded with visitors. This is because there will be activities to celebrate the 21st anniversary of the province of Bangka Belitung, as well as various office and central government events brought to Belitung.

Tanjung Bira is located about 40 km from Bulukumba City or 200 km from Makassar City and takes about 3-4 hours. Not only known for the charm of the beauty of its beaches, Tanjung Bira is also a traditional South Sulawesi shipbuilding house and visitors can see the shipbuilding process on the beach. There are many things that visitors can do in the Tanjung Bira tourist area, one of which is snorkeling and diving while enjoying the underwater beauty at several spots on this beach. The spot around Liukang Loe Island, which is a small island located opposite the west of Tanjung Bira, is one of the best spots. Visitors can also spend their holidays at Tanjung Bira by just basking in the sun while enjoying the fresh breeze that blows and watching the enchanting sunrise and sunset.
