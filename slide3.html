The Ampera Bridge is the Icon building of the city of Palembang. It is red and crosses the Musi river. Many tourists take photos against the background of this bridge. The Palembang city government has arranged a very good place for visitors to flock to this area, look at and take pictures with the graceful backdrop of the Ampera Bridge. There are also restaurants here to eat and drink after getting tired of enjoying the beauty of this bridge.

Tanjung Bira


Click here if you want to know more about Tanjung Bira

Tanjung Bira is located about 40 km from Bulukumba City or 200 km from Makassar City and takes about 3-4 hours. Not only known for the charm of the beauty of its beaches, Tanjung Bira is also a traditional South Sulawesi shipbuilding house and visitors can see the shipbuilding process on the beach. There are many things that visitors can do in the Tanjung Bira tourist area, one of which is snorkeling and diving while enjoying the underwater beauty at several spots on this beach. The spot around Liukang Loe Island, which is a small island located opposite the west of Tanjung Bira, is one of the best spots. Visitors can also spend their holiday time at Tanjung Bira just basking in the sun while enjoying the fresh breeze that blows and watching the enchanting sunrise and sunset.
